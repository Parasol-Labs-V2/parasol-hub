# Spectrum Internet: Use Case by Job Function

## Target: Lookalike Campaigns for Internet Service Provider Workforce

---

## Job Functions & Parasol Use Cases

### 1. Field Technicians
**Role:** Install, maintain, and expand fiber broadband network at customer premises

| Pain Point | Parasol Solution |
|------------|------------------|
| High turnover in physically demanding roles | Target experienced technicians seeking stability |
| Constant hiring need across 41 states | Geo-targeted lookalike campaigns by market |
| Need CDL/technical certifications | Filter for qualified candidates with relevant credentials |
| Safety-conscious culture fit | Behavioral targeting for reliability indicators |

**Scale:** Primary hiring focus with continuous open requisitions nationwide

---

### 2. Customer Service Representatives
**Role:** Call center experts guiding customers to connectivity solutions

| Pain Point | Parasol Solution |
|------------|------------------|
| High-volume seasonal hiring | Surge campaigns aligned with promotional periods |
| Bilingual requirements in key markets | Language-targeted lookalikes (Spanish, Mandarin) |
| Work-from-home flexibility needs | Target remote-ready candidates with home office setup |
| Retention challenges in call centers | Lookalikes based on tenure indicators |

**Scale:** 100% U.S.-based workforce requirement creates domestic hiring premium

---

### 3. Sales (Residential & SMB)
**Role:** Inside, outside, and retail sales connecting customers with Spectrum products

| Pain Point | Parasol Solution |
|------------|------------------|
| Commission-driven role requires self-starters | Target high-activity, goal-oriented profiles |
| Door-to-door/field sales positions | Geo-fence competitor retail locations |
| Retail store staffing across markets | Mall/shopping center proximity targeting |
| Upsell/cross-sell skills needed | Lookalikes from telecom/wireless sales |

**Scale:** Multi-channel sales force spanning retail, field, and telesales

---

### 4. Enterprise Sales
**Role:** B2B solutions for large business accounts

| Pain Point | Parasol Solution |
|------------|------------------|
| Complex technical sales cycle | Target candidates with B2B telecom/IT background |
| Relationship-driven, long sales cycles | LinkedIn lookalikes from enterprise tech sales |
| Territory management required | Regional targeting with account-based signals |

**Scale:** Strategic hires in major metro markets

---

### 5. Network Operations & Engineering
**Role:** Backend infrastructure, NOC, and technical support

| Pain Point | Parasol Solution |
|------------|------------------|
| Competitive market for network engineers | Target passive candidates from competitor NOCs |
| 24/7 operations staffing | Shift-flexible candidate targeting |
| Specialized certifications (CCNA, CCNP) | Credential-based lookalike audiences |

**Scale:** Critical infrastructure roles in regional hubs

---

# Company Overview: Spectrum / Charter Communications

## Corporate Profile

**Parent Company:** Charter Communications, Inc. (NASDAQ: CHTR)  
**Headquarters:** 400 Washington Blvd., Stamford, CT  
**Consumer Brand:** Spectrum (internet, TV, mobile, voice)  
**Service Footprint:** 41 states, ~59 million homes and businesses  
**Employees:** 100,000+ (100% U.S.-based workforce)

---
